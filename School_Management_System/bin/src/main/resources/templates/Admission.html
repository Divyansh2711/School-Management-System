ADMISSION PROCEDURE


     
  1. For online admission process, Application form has to be filled online under admission link on website. Application & Prospectus Charges are non refundable.
  2. On successfully submitting the Application form, a unique Registration key (Application id) will be generated and communicated to parent through email & SMS.
  3. On successful submission of Application form, parent will be informed through Email & SMS about date and time to visit the campus for interaction & verification of original documents.
  4. On selection for admission, parent will receive a confirmation through Email & SMS.
  5. Parent is required to complete the Enrollment formalities by remitting the "Amount payable at the time of admission".
  6. Amount payable at the time of admission confirmation has to be deposited through online payment i.e. Netbanking /Debit / Credit Card.

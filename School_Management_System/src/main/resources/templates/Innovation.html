Innovative Practices


The overarching theme of the education policies is to achieve quality and innovation and to move from rote learning to knowledge transfer. To inculcate the same, at Delhi Public School-Delhi, the curriculum is structured to include Innovative activities, which cater to Multiple intelligences and inculcate 21st century skills. The concept of Blended Learning is incorporated through ICT Tools (Information and computer technologies) to create e-learning classrooms.

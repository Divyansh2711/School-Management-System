Senior Secondary Curriculum


CLASSES XI


  1. Promotion is granted on the basis of the whole year’s work of the student; hence regularity in attendance and work is imperative.

  2. Promotion of the students at the end of the academic session : (Class XI) is as per CBSE bye-laws.

    1. The academic session is divided into 2 terms – Each Term will have 50% weightage.

    2. In the 1st Term, one round of Block test, Worksheets and Half-yearly Examination are planned to be conducted.

    3. In the 2nd Term, 1 round of Block Exam, Practice worksheets and Annual Exam will be conducted.

CLASSES XII


  1. In the 1st Term, one round of Block Tests, Worksheets and Half-yearly examination are planned to be conducted.

  2. In Term II, Class XII has Pre-Board-1 Exam in December and Pre-Board-2 Exam in January from the entire syllabus.
